# Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Frame Forge: Storytelling Studio, also identified in the project as Comics-I did, package `com.sgxp.frameforge`.

## Summary

Frame Forge is an offline comic staging and page layout app. The current version does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

## Information the App Stores

The app stores comic project data locally on your device so that your projects can be reopened and edited. This may include:

- project names entered by you;
- project history, such as the most recently opened project;
- comic scene data, character setup, pose data, camera shot data, page layout data, and print settings;
- generated or selected frame image paths used by your comic pages.

This data is stored in the app's local Godot user data folder on your device. It is not sent to the app developer by the app.

## Information the App Does Not Collect

The current version of the app does not collect:

- names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account identifiers;
- location data;
- contacts, calendar data, photos, videos, or media library content;
- camera, microphone, or audio recordings from the device;
- advertising identifiers;
- analytics, diagnostics, crash reports, or telemetry;
- payment information.

## Internet Access and Third Parties

The current Android build does not request Internet permission and does not include advertising, analytics, cloud sync, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Because the app does not send data over the Internet, the app does not share user data with third parties.

## Device Permissions

The current Android build does not request sensitive Android permissions such as location, camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, external storage, media access, notifications, or Internet access.

## Data Deletion

Project data is stored locally on your device. You can delete app data through your device's app settings or by uninstalling the app. The current Android export is configured not to retain app data after uninstall.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. Because the app does not collect or transmit personal information, there is no personal information for the app developer to review or delete.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app adds online features, analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync, account support, ads, in-app purchases, or new device permissions. The updated policy will be published in this repository or in another location made available with the app.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact method provided on the app's GitHub repository or app store listing.
